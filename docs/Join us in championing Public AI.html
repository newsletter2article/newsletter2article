Championing ‚ÄòPublic AI‚Äô with Mozilla Common Voice

‘Public AI’ projects are advancing openly developed artificial intelligence created through collaboration to serve the public good, prioritizing societal benefits over commercial profit. These projects are central to Mozilla’s mission to make technology more inclusive and accessible. Leading the way is Mozilla Common Voice, an open-source initiative that crowdsources multilingual speech and text data. High-quality data is often a bottleneck in adapting AI models for specialized uses, but Common Voice is changing that by enabling developers and communities around the world to create voice-enabled applications, making it easier for more people to use technology. Open source data sets on Mozilla Common Voice are critical for driving our vision of Public AI. Read more about our new vision for Public AI.

Want to support Public AI? One way you can help is by donating your data and time to trustworthy AI projects like Common Voice. We’re also looking for Language Community Champions who can make informed decisions about language-specific questions and concerns. As our platform grows to support over 120 languages, your donations and volunteering can ensure your language is accurately represented and help unlock the benefits of AI for your community.
